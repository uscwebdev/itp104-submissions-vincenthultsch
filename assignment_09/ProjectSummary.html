Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce a consumer who is thinking of environmental-friendly issues to the world of SustainablyStyled - a non fast fashion brand - that is attempting to disrupt the clothes marketplace

The website attempts to give context on the story of the company, its commitment to maintaining and constantly improving practices as well as commitments made by the company to ensure the health of the planet

The website also contains a way for consumers to view some of the options of different tops, bottoms, and other good that are sold.

Extras

Extra 1: Iframe - Iframe used to load in video on the homepage html in order to provide some video content for the user, it was a common thing on the inspirations I looked at for the model and I felt the video contributed to the theme and design of the website

Extra 2: HTML5 Media Tags - audiofile created using text to speech tools that is used as a testimonial about the working conditions, overall company culture and commitment to practices

Extra 3: Form Submission - suggestion box for consumers to send to the company, or ask questions works and sends the user to a confirmation page before asking them go return to homepage
